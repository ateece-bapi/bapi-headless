## Executive Summary

Belimo is BAPI's largest competitor and the established market leader with 50 years of industry presence. While they have strong brand recognition, extensive product portfolio, and corporate resources, their website suffers from information overload and outdated user experience. BAPI has significant opportunities to compete through superior digital experience, faster information access, and modern technical resources.

**Key Takeaway:** Belimo owns the traditional B2B market through relationships and trust. BAPI can win by being faster, cleaner, and more helpful for the modern engineer who values speed and accessibility.
