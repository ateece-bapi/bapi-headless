## Executive Summary

The building automation market consists of multiple layers, and BAPI operates in the **field device/sensor layer** - not the building management system (BMS) software layer. Understanding this distinction is critical for strategic positioning.

### Key Findings

1. **Direct Competitor:** Belimo (field devices - actuators, valves, sensors)
2. **Integration Partners:** Siemens, Honeywell, Automated Logic (BMS platforms that need sensors)
3. **Market Opportunity:** Most BMS vendors need quality third-party sensors
4. **BAPI's Advantage:** Specialized sensor expertise, modern digital presence, superior documentation

### Strategic Positioning

> **"Best-in-class sensors that integrate seamlessly with any building automation platform. We make Siemens, Honeywell, Automated Logic, and other BMS systems perform better."**

**Core Strategy:**
- ‚úÖ Compete with Belimo on device quality and digital experience
- ‚úÖ Partner with BMS vendors for integration and specifications
- ‚úÖ Target engineers and specifiers with superior technical resources
- ‚úÖ Win through speed, accessibility, and modern user experience

### Market Layers Explained

**Layer 1: Enterprise Software** (Top)
- **Players:** Siemens Building X, Honeywell Forge, etc.
- **Function:** Portfolio management, analytics, reporting
- **Buyers:** Corporate real estate, facility directors
- **Not relevant to BAPI:** Too high level

**Layer 2: Building Management Systems** (Middle) ‚≠ê INTEGRATION TARGET
- **Players:** Siemens, Honeywell, Automated Logic, Johnson Controls, Schneider
- **Function:** Building control, HVAC management, integration platform
- **Buyers:** Facility managers, building owners, system integrators
- **BAPI Relationship:** Integration partners - they need our sensors

**Layer 3: Field Devices** (Bottom) ‚≠ê BAPI'S MARKET
- **Players:** BAPI, Belimo, plus BMS vendor devices
- **Function:** Data collection, actuation, control
- **Buyers:** Engineers, contractors, integrators
- **BAPI Relationship:** Direct sales - this is our market
